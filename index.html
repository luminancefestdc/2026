The Feast

Where a hundred brass lamps
are lit as one

Every monsoon's end, DCSMAT turns its courtyard into the state's largest student gathering — equal parts sadya, stage and spectacle. Luminous Fest brings together performers, chefs, makers and dreamers from across Kerala's colleges for three days of competitions, cultural nights and a feast served the traditional way: on banana leaf, under a thousand flickering flames.
